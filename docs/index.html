China Travel Preparation Checklist

Your comprehensive guide to preparing for your trip to China

Visa and Documentation

  • Apply for Chinese visa at least 1 month before travel
  • Ensure passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond stay
  • Make copies of important documents (passport, visa, travel insurance)
  • Keep digital copies in cloud storage

Internet and Communication

  • Download essential apps before leaving (WeChat, Alipay, Maps)
  • Set up VPN for accessing international websites
  • Purchase local SIM card or international roaming plan
  • Install translation apps (Pleco, Google Translate)

Money and Payments

  • Set up WeChat Pay or Alipay with international card
  • Carry some cash (RMB) for emergencies
  • Notify your bank about international travel
  • Keep emergency contact numbers and card details

Transportation

  • Download local transportation apps (Didi, local metro apps)
  • Book airport transfers in advance
  • Research public transportation options
  • Save important addresses in Chinese characters

Accommodation

  • Book hotels through international platforms
  • Save hotel address in Chinese
  • Check if hotel accepts foreign cards
  • Confirm check-in requirements

Health and Safety

  • Get necessary vaccinations
  • Pack basic medical supplies
  • Purchase travel insurance
  • Save emergency numbers

Cultural Preparation

  • Learn basic Chinese phrases
  • Research local customs and etiquette
  • Download offline maps
  • Book popular attractions in advance
